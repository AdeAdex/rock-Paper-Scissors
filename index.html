Welcome to the rock, Paper, scissors game rules and regulations

Game Instructions

Overview

This game is played by children and adults and is popular all over the world. Apart from being a game played to pass time, the game is usually played in situations where something has to be chosen. It is similar in that way to other games like flipping the coin, throwing dice or drawing straws. There is no room for cheating or for knowing what the other person is going to do so the results are usually very satisfying with no room for fighting or error.

How To Play

Before playing the game, ensure you register a name for yourself and your Opponent (Computer) then:

Make your choice by clicking on any of the three (3) button (Rock, Paper or Scissors ) and upon any of your choice, computer choice will be generated automatically.

The winner of the first round will be decided after five (5) click. Meaning that if you click any of the three buttons for the first time, both your choice and computer choice is recorded and for you to know who beat the other on the first, second, third, fourth and fifth click, a floating icon is displayed with +1 which denotes that the user that got the +1 icon is gaining advantage of winning the game after the fifth click , and until after the fifth time of click, then the winner is decided checking who has the highest +1 icon and the winner is congratulated

Note: After the first round, every score is reset and the next round start following the same rules of how to play. This game as at the moment of developing it, doesn't support multiple players, it can only be played by one player and the opponent as Computer.

Instructions

Rock Paper Scissors is a two player game. The objective is to select a play (i.e Rock, Paper or Scissors) that defeat your opponent's play
  • If one player picks rock and one scissors, the player who pick Rock wins the dispute

    Rock Smashes Scissors, Rock Win

  • If one player picks scissors and the other paper, the player who showed scissors succeeds.

    Scissors cut Paper, Scissors Win

  • If a player shows paper while the other shows rock, the player who picked paper succeeds.

    Paper covers Rock, Paper Win

  • If the play is the same, nobody win, It's a tie.

    Play again to win
